# Payroll Report FY2026

## What is the overview of the payroll report?
This document contains comprehensive compensation details for all Aether Corp employees for the 2026 fiscal year. It provides a detailed breakdown of salary bands, individual compensation packages, and overall payroll expenses. This information is critical for financial planning and budgeting purposes.

## What are the standard base salary bands by engineering role?
Our standard base salary bands are strictly defined by role. A Junior Engineer can expect a base salary ranging from 60,000 USD to 80,000 USD. A Senior Engineer is compensated between 110,000 USD and 150,000 USD. Staff Engineers receive between 160,000 USD and 200,000 USD. Finally, an Engineering Lead commands a base salary between 180,000 USD and 240,000 USD.

## What is the structure for variable and retention bonuses?
Our compensation strategy includes variable bonus structures. Employees are eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to twenty percent of their base salary, dependent on individual and company achievements. We also utilize targeted retention bonuses, which are negotiated and awarded on a strictly case by case basis.

## How is equity distributed across roles?
Equity is a core component of our compensation philosophy. Standard engineers receive equity grants ranging from 0.05 percent to 0.2 percent. Engineering and functional leads are granted between 0.2 percent and 0.5 percent. Executive leadership positions are awarded equity stakes of 1 percent or higher.

## What are the projected monthly and annual payroll expenses?
Our current financial modeling indicates significant ongoing payroll expenses. The projected monthly payroll expenditure is approximately 1.8 million USD. When annualized, this represents a total projected annual payroll obligation of roughly 21.6 million USD.

## What are the confidentiality notes regarding this payroll document?
This document is strictly confidential and represents sensitive financial data. It must not be shared outside of explicitly authorized roles within the finance and executive departments. Unauthorized distribution is grounds for immediate termination.

## What is the strict schedule for payroll processing?
Payroll is processed on a semi monthly basis, specifically on the fifteenth and the final day of every month. If a payday falls on a weekend or a recognized bank holiday, funds will be disbursed on the preceding business day. The finance team requires all expense reports to be submitted three days prior to the payroll run.

## How do we handle tax withholdings across different jurisdictions?
Because we employ a distributed remote workforce, we utilize a global employer of record service to manage complex tax compliance. This service ensures that all local, state, and federal taxes are correctly calculated and withheld based on the employee's primary legal residence.

## What is the formal process for requesting a compensation review?
Compensation reviews are formally conducted during the annual performance cycle. However, an out of cycle review can be requested by an employee's direct manager if there has been a significant change in the scope of their responsibilities. These requests must be approved by both the HR Lead and the Chief Financial Officer.

## What are the standard severance package guidelines?
In the event of a non cause termination, we provide a standard severance package consisting of four weeks of base pay, plus one additional week for every full year of tenure at the company. We also cover the cost of continued health insurance premiums for a period of two months following departure.

## How are relocation stipends calculated and distributed?
If an employee is required to relocate to be closer to a strategic hub, we offer a relocation stipend. The standard amount is 10,000 USD for domestic moves and 20,000 USD for international relocations. This amount is paid out as a lump sum on the first paycheck following the move and is subject to standard tax withholdings.

## What is the impact of unpaid leave on equity vesting schedules?
If an employee takes a continuous unpaid leave of absence exceeding thirty days, their equity vesting schedule will be paused. Vesting will resume immediately upon their official return to active full time status. Standard paid time off and parental leave do not impact the vesting schedule.

## How do we urgently handle payroll anomalies or system errors?
Any discrepancies in a paycheck must be reported immediately to the finance department via a secure internal ticketing system. We guarantee that any verified underpayment caused by an administrative error will be corrected and disbursed within forty eight hours via an expedited wire transfer.

## What is the allocated budget for team building events?
To foster team cohesion in a remote environment, each department is allocated a quarterly team building budget. This budget is calculated at 250 USD per headcount per quarter. Managers have full discretion on how to utilize these funds for virtual events, offsite meetings, or shared meals.

## What are the rigorous financial auditing procedures?
To ensure the integrity of our payroll system, we conduct a comprehensive internal audit every six months. Additionally, we engage an independent accounting firm annually to perform a full audit of all our financial records, including executive compensation and equity distribution, ensuring complete regulatory compliance.

## What is the specific policy for issuing signing bonuses?
Signing bonuses are not standard practice and are reserved strictly for highly competitive senior engineering and executive roles. Any signing bonus exceeding 15,000 USD must include a one year clawback provision, meaning the employee must repay a prorated amount if they depart the company within their first twelve months.
