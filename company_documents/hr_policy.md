# HR Policies and Hiring Guide

## What is our hiring philosophy?
Our hiring philosophy is the foundation of our company culture. We prioritize extreme talent density, seeking out only the most capable individuals in their respective fields. We strictly evaluate for cultural alignment, ensuring that new hires embody our core values. Above all, we look for an ownership mindset, where candidates demonstrate a history of taking profound responsibility for their work.

## What is our four step hiring process?
We employ a rigorous and structured four step hiring process. First, we conduct a thorough resume screening to identify candidates who meet our baseline qualifications. Second, candidates undergo a demanding technical interview to validate their hard skills. Third, we hold a culture fit interview to assess their alignment with our values. Finally, all potential hires must pass a final executive review before an offer is extended.

## What are our compensation guidelines and equity policy?
Our compensation packages are designed to attract and retain elite talent. We consistently offer salaries within established, competitive salary bands. We dynamically adjust these offers based on the candidate's specific experience and market value. Furthermore, we systematically include significant equity grants for all senior roles to align long term incentives.

## How is performance management conducted?
Performance management at Aether Corp is an ongoing dialogue. We conduct formal quarterly check ins to ensure alignment on goals and provide timely feedback. These culminate in comprehensive annual reviews that determine overall performance ratings. Based on these reviews, we hold structured promotion cycles to recognize and reward exceptional contributions.

## How do we ensure employee retention?
We invest heavily in retaining our top performers. We maintain highly competitive salaries that reflect the current market conditions. We actively provide structured growth opportunities and clear paths for career advancement. Furthermore, we believe that transparent leadership and open communication are critical for maintaining high employee morale and loyalty.

## How do we handle conflict resolution?
We aim to resolve interpersonal conflicts swiftly and professionally. Our first line of resolution involves structured mediation facilitated by the Human Resources department. If a conflict cannot be resolved at this level, we have a clear process for escalation to senior leadership for a final, binding decision.

## What is our termination policy?
Employment termination is handled with clarity and decisiveness. We maintain a clear policy for performance based termination, which occurs after a documented period of inadequate improvement. Conversely, we execute immediate termination for severe violations of our code of conduct or security policies.

## What is our stance on diversity and inclusion?
We actively aim to build a diverse and inclusive workplace across all teams. We believe that a variety of perspectives and backgrounds is essential for driving innovation and making robust business decisions. We are committed to fostering an environment where everyone feels welcomed and empowered to contribute.

## What is the process for creating a new role?
Managers must submit a detailed business justification to the HR department before a new headcount is approved. This justification must include the expected responsibilities, required skills, and the direct impact the role will have on our strategic objectives. Once approved, the talent acquisition team will begin the sourcing process.

## How do we handle reference checks during hiring?
We conduct extensive reference checks for all final stage candidates. We require at least three professional references, including one former manager. We use these conversations to validate the candidate's past performance, verify their claims, and gain insights into how best to manage them.

## What is the policy for hiring contractors versus full time employees?
We prioritize full time employment for all core engineering and strategic roles. We utilize contractors strictly for short term, highly specialized projects or to temporarily augment our capacity during peak periods. Contractors are subject to the same security requirements as full time staff.

## How do we handle employee referrals and referral bonuses?
We highly encourage employee referrals as they are consistently our best source of talent. If a referred candidate is hired and remains with the company for ninety days, the referring employee will receive a substantial cash bonus. The specific amount varies depending on the seniority of the filled role.

## What is the visa sponsorship policy for international hires?
We are committed to securing the best talent globally and will sponsor employment visas for exceptionally qualified candidates. The HR department works directly with external legal counsel to navigate the immigration process. We cover all legal and filing fees associated with securing the initial work authorization.

## How do we manage workplace accommodations?
We are dedicated to providing reasonable accommodations to employees with disabilities or specific medical needs. Employees should submit an accommodation request directly to HR. We will work collaboratively to implement solutions that enable the employee to perform their duties effectively and comfortably.

## What is the policy for rehiring former employees?
We welcome the return of former employees who left the company in good standing. We refer to these individuals as boomerang employees. They must undergo a streamlined interview process to ensure their skills are still aligned with our current needs, but they are generally given high priority in the candidate pool.

## What are the guidelines for conducting exit interviews?
Every departing employee is required to complete an exit interview with an HR representative. We use this structured conversation to gather candid feedback on their experience at the company, their reasons for leaving, and their perspective on the effectiveness of our leadership team.

## What is our approach to continuous talent mapping?
Our talent acquisition team does not simply wait for open roles. They engage in continuous talent mapping to identify passive candidates who would be exceptional additions to our team in the future. We proactively build relationships with industry leaders long before we have a specific requisition open for them.

## How do we handle reductions in force or layoffs?
While we strive to avoid them through prudent financial planning, reductions in force are sometimes necessary. If this occurs, we execute the process with the utmost respect and transparency. Affected employees receive generous severance packages, extended healthcare coverage, and dedicated outplacement support services.
