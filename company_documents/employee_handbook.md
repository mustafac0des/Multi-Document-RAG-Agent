# Aether Corp Employee Handbook

## What is our mission and welcome to Aether Corp?
Aether Corp is an AI infrastructure company focused on building scalable systems for intelligent applications. Our mission is to empower developers and enterprises to deploy AI reliably at scale. We are thrilled to have you join our team and contribute to this ambitious vision. Your work here will directly impact how the world interacts with and benefits from artificial intelligence.

## What is our company value of Ownership?
Every employee is expected to take full ownership of their work. This means seeing a project through from conception to completion, anticipating potential roadblocks, and taking proactive steps to overcome them. You are empowered to make decisions and are accountable for the outcomes.

## What is our company value of Speed?
We prioritize rapid iteration and continuous delivery. The AI landscape moves incredibly fast, and our ability to outpace the competition relies on our agility. We value quick decision making and the willingness to ship early and iterate based on real world feedback.

## What is our company value of Transparency?
Information flows openly unless restricted for legal or strategic reasons. We believe that a well informed team is an empowered team. We share our successes and our failures openly, fostering a culture of trust and collective learning.

## What is our Code of Conduct?
All employees are expected to adhere to our code of conduct. You must maintain professionalism in all interactions, both internal and external. We demand that you respect diversity and foster an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued. Furthermore, you must proactively avoid any conflicts of interest that could compromise your judgment or the company's integrity.

## What is our remote policy for the work environment?
Aether Corp operates as a remote first company. We believe that great talent can be found anywhere in the world. Our remote policy is designed to provide you with the flexibility to work from wherever you are most productive.

## What are the required working hours?
We offer flexible working hours to accommodate different time zones and personal schedules. However, we require that all teams maintain a four hour overlap during their workday to facilitate synchronous communication and collaboration.

## What are our comprehensive benefits, stipends, and PTO?
We provide comprehensive benefits to ensure your well being and professional growth. This includes global health insurance coverage to keep you and your family healthy. We also offer an annual learning stipend of 2000 USD to support your continuous education and skill development. Additionally, you will receive 20 days of paid time off per year to rest and recharge.

## What is required for security awareness?
Security is everyone's responsibility at Aether Corp. You must never share your access credentials with anyone, under any circumstances. You are required to promptly report any suspicious activity or potential security incidents to the appropriate team. Furthermore, you must strictly follow all data classification policies to ensure sensitive information is protected.

## What is the data classification overview?
Our data classification system consists of four primary levels. The Public level is open to all individuals, both inside and outside the company. The Internal level is restricted to Aether Corp employees only. The Sensitive level is strictly limited to authorized members of restricted departments. Finally, the Restricted level contains highly confidential information and is accessible exclusively by the executive team.

## What communication tools do we use?
We utilize a specific set of tools for our daily operations. Slack is our primary platform for internal, real time communication. We use Notion extensively for all internal documentation and knowledge sharing. For version control and collaborative code development, we rely on GitHub.

## How are performance reviews conducted?
Performance reviews are conducted bi annually to provide structured feedback and support your career progression. These reviews incorporate peer feedback to provide a well rounded perspective on your contributions. Your manager will also provide a comprehensive evaluation of your performance. Finally, you will complete a self assessment to reflect on your own achievements and areas for growth.

## What is the exit policy?
If you choose to leave Aether Corp, you must follow our structured exit policy. You are required to return all company assets, including laptops and other hardware. All of your access credentials will be immediately revoked. Finally, you must complete a thorough knowledge transfer process to ensure a smooth transition for your team.

## What is the onboarding process for new hires?
The onboarding process is designed to integrate you smoothly into the company. During your first week, you will complete essential security training and tool setup. You will be assigned a buddy who will help you navigate the company culture and answer any immediate questions. By the end of your first month, you are expected to ship your first piece of code or complete your first major project deliverable.

## How do we handle expenses and reimbursements?
Employees are encouraged to use company issued credit cards for all necessary business expenses. If you must use personal funds, you can submit an expense report through our finance portal. All reimbursements must include itemized receipts and a clear business justification. Approvals are typically processed within five business days.

## What is the policy on side projects?
We encourage intellectual curiosity and side projects as long as they do not conflict with our business interests. However, you must explicitly declare any commercial side projects to the HR department. You are strictly prohibited from using company equipment, intellectual property, or confidential information for any external ventures.

## What are the guidelines for social media usage?
You are welcome to mention your employment at Aether Corp on personal social media accounts. However, you must make it clear that your opinions are your own and do not represent the company. You are strictly forbidden from sharing any confidential information, internal screenshots, or unreleased product details on any public platform.

## How do we support employee mental health?
We recognize that building the future of AI is demanding work. We provide access to confidential counseling services through our health insurance provider. We also encourage employees to take mental health days as needed, which count towards your standard paid time off balance.

## What is the parental leave policy?
We offer generous parental leave to support growing families. All eligible employees receive sixteen weeks of fully paid leave following the birth or adoption of a child. This leave can be taken consecutively or split into smaller blocks within the first year of the child's arrival.

## What are the guidelines for company retreats?
We believe in the power of face to face connection. We host an annual company wide retreat to build relationships and align on our strategic vision. Attendance is highly encouraged but not mandatory. All travel and accommodation expenses for these events are fully covered by the company.

## How do we handle internal transfers and promotions?
We prioritize internal mobility and career growth. If you are interested in a different role within the company, you are encouraged to discuss it with your manager. Internal transfers require you to have been in your current role for at least one year and to be in good standing regarding performance.

## What is the policy for office equipment and hardware?
Every new employee receives a top of the line laptop and a generous stipend to outfit their home office. You are responsible for maintaining this equipment in good working order. Upgrades are evaluated on a three year cycle or sooner if required for specific technical tasks.

## What is our stance on continuous learning?
We expect every employee to dedicate time to expanding their skill set. Your annual learning stipend can be used for conferences, online courses, or relevant books. We also host internal tech talks every month where team members share their knowledge on emerging technologies.

## What are the procedures for filing a grievance?
If you experience harassment, discrimination, or any violation of our code of conduct, you should immediately file a grievance with the HR department. We take all reports seriously and will conduct a prompt, impartial investigation while maintaining strict confidentiality to protect all parties involved.
